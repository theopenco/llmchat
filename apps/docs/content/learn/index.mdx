Everything you do in Clanker Support — triaging conversations, tuning your support agent, growing its knowledge base — happens in the dashboard at [app.clankersupport.com](https://app.clankersupport.com). Every page shares the same chrome: a top bar for switching context, a sidebar for navigation, and an account menu. This page walks through that chrome so the rest of this section reads without surprises. If you haven't created an agent yet, start with [Getting started](/getting-started).

## Top bar

| Control                | What it does                                                                                                                                         |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Workspace switcher** | Switches between the workspaces you belong to. Each workspace has its own projects, members, and billing — see [Workspaces & members](./workspaces). |
| **Project switcher**   | Selects the project you're working on and jumps to its pages. Hidden until the workspace has at least one project.                                   |
| **Search**             | Opens the command palette — also on **⌘K** / **Ctrl+K**. See [Search & command palette](./search).                                                   |
| **Notification bell**  | A workspace-wide feed of new conversations, escalations, and visitor replies. See [Notifications](./notifications).                                  |
| **Help**               | Opens the documentation in a new tab.                                                                                                                |
| **Account menu**       | Your initials avatar — profile, billing, appearance, and sign out (see below).                                                                       |

## Sidebar

The sidebar has two groups:

- **Workspace** — **Conversations** opens the [Inbox](./inbox); **Projects** opens the [projects list](./projects).
- **Project** — **Sources** and **Settings** for the selected project: its [knowledge sources](./sources) and [project settings](./project-settings). This group appears once a project is selected.

At the bottom, the **usage meter** shows the workspace's plan and how many AI responses it has used this billing period — the same numbers as the [Billing](./billing) page.

Until the workspace is on a plan, an amber banner reminds you that your agent isn't live yet; **Choose a plan** takes you to the [Billing](./billing) page to pick one.

## Account menu

Click your avatar in the top-right corner. The menu shows the email you're signed in with and your workspace role, and contains:

- **Account** — your profile and account settings. See [Account](./account).
- **Billing** — the workspace's plan and usage. See [Billing](./billing).
- **Appearance** — switch the dashboard between **Light**, **Dark**, and **System** (follows your OS setting).
- **Sign out** — end your session.
