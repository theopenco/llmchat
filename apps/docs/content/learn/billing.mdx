Billing lives at **Settings → Billing** — open it from **Billing** in the account menu or the usage meter at the bottom of the sidebar. Hosted Clanker Support is usage-based: every AI response your agent sends counts toward your plan's monthly quota, and this page is where you watch that number, choose a plan, and manage the subscription.

## Your current plan

The card at the top reflects your workspace's live state:

| Item                      | What it shows                                                                                                                      |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Plan**                  | Your current tier and its price                                                                                                    |
| **Manage in Stripe**      | Opens the Stripe billing portal — update your payment method, download invoices, or cancel. Owner only                             |
| **Responses this period** | AI responses sent since the start of the current calendar month (UTC)                                                              |
| **Plan limit bar**        | Your progress toward the AI responses included in your plan, so you can see when you're approaching — or past — the included quota |

The usage meter at the bottom of the sidebar mirrors the same plan and response count from anywhere in the dashboard.

## Choosing a plan

Three tiers are available: **Starter**, **Growth** (Most popular), and **Scale**. Use the **Monthly / Annual** toggle to switch the billing cadence — annual works out to roughly two months free, and each card shows the exact yearly total before you commit.

Plans differ in:

- Included AI responses per month
- What happens past the included quota — some tiers meter overage billed per response, others state a hard cap (each plan card says which)
- Number of projects and team seats
- Model access (fast models vs. all models)
- Widget branding (whether the "Powered by" badge shows)

Current limits and prices are listed on each plan card and on the [pricing page](https://clankersupport.com/pricing).

## What happens at checkout

1. Click **Choose &lt;tier&gt;** — you're redirected to Stripe Checkout.
2. Enter payment details. A card is required to start; there is no free tier.
3. Once Stripe confirms the payment, your workspace is upgraded automatically and your agent can respond.

Billing is **owner-only**: only the workspace owner can start a checkout, change the plan, or open the billing portal. Other members can view the page, but the buttons are disabled with a note.

Until a workspace has an active plan, its agent isn't live — the dashboard shows an amber banner and onboarding starts with the plan picker. See [Getting started](/getting-started).

## Where usage comes from

- Each AI response the agent generates in the widget counts as **one response**, recorded the moment it's produced.
- Replies your teammates send from the inbox are human replies, not AI responses — they are never metered.
- The **Responses this period** counter resets at the start of each calendar month (UTC).
- On tiers with overage, responses beyond the included quota are metered and billed per response through Stripe; the current-plan card calls out how far over you are.
